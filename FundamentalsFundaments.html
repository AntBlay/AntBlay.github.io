Fundamental's fundaments

General process

In order to use Fundamental, the investor must imagine a serie of steps to go from the whole stock market to a tiny group of stocks to pick to constitute a portfolio. The investor will associate each step with a group of indicator.

When using Fundamental, the application will display, for each step, a list of stocks with the associated indicators. The investor will be able to select a list of stock based on the indicator value. This stock selection will be the entry point to display next step indicators.
